Settings

From here, you will be able to choose whether you want an overall time goal (e.g. 15 minutes), or a static time-per-person.

Total Max Time
If the Total Max Time checkbox is selected, the time (in seconds) is used to define the max length of the Scrum meeting. The time for each participant is scaled to fit. If it is left unchecked, each participant is given the time shown in seconds.
Remote Participant list
